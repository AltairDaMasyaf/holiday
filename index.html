About

Holiday is a token project created on the Binance Smart Chain. The purpose of Holiday is to create its own holiday ecosystem and use it as a payment tool.


It has 3 different prize system:


  • Hold to Earn.
  • Stake to Earn.
  • Travel to Earn.


In addition, it is to provide accommodation opportunities to low-income people living in various parts of the world in the most popular holiday destinations with lotteries and various prize pool transactions.


With the power of the community it creates the most popular holiday project in the world by developing periodical events, sightseeing tours and competitions.
